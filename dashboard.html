This document contains a summary with preliminary results from the TI-LGAD characterization at UZH. Some of these results were presented at the RD50 workshop, see here.

Collected charge

Gain

The gain was calculated as the ratio between the collected charge of the respective device and the reference measurement with a PIN diode. The collected charge of the PIN diode was linearly interpolated to obtain values in between.

Time resolution

The time resolution in the plot below is $\frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{2}}$ using the constant fraction discriminator method, measured with an infrared laser (so missing Landau).

Annealing

One family of detectors was left for annealing at room temperature during 7 days. Below there are some plots where systematic changes could be identified. IPD showed no systematic modifications.

Annealing effect on IV curves

Annealing effect on collected charge and gain

Devices seem to reduce their gain after annealing.

Annealing effect on time resolution

The time resolution seems to be slightly worsened by annealing. This is probably related to the observed reduction in the gain.
